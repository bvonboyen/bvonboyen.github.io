Innovation in risk & value management — with people and intelligent tooling

If you’re driving transformation in risk management, rethinking pension design, or advancing analytics and AI in practice, you’ll find here a mix of shared ideas, quantitative insights, and hands-on examples from real projects, as well as my professional background.
